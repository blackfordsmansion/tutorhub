Chess Curriculum

  • **Fundamentals:** Learn the rules, how the pieces move, and basic checkmate patterns.
  • **Opening Principles:** Discover the goals of the opening phase and common strategies.
  • **Tactics:** Master essential tactics like forks, pins, skewers, and discoveries.
  • **Endgame:** Study fundamental endgames and learn to convert advantages.
  • **Strategy:** Develop a deeper understanding of positional play, pawn structures, and long-term planning.
